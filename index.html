About Me


I have been teaching English for the past 10 years in Taiwan. Currently, I am retraining and working towards a BSC in Computer Science through the University of London (Goldsmiths). I am looking to build experience in the software sector and gain expertise through positive work interactions.

I have completed two courses from Harvard University, CS50x, which gives a broad and in-depth look at computer science, and CS50w, which is their course on web development using Javascript and Python. Through these, I have created a couple of projects to showcase the skills that I have learnt, the second of which is live at www.footballgoalpool.com. It's a fantasy football game built from the ground up in Django, that uses both Python on the backend and JavaScript on the front end.

With over a decade of experience teaching, I have developed a range of soft skills that can translate seamlessly into different work environments. I'm comfortable taking a leading role, but not scared to ask questions and learn from those more experienced than me. Taking this step to transition at this stage in my career has been a challenge, but it is a challenge that I am relishing. I have the enthusiasm to learn and prove myself in an exciting new industry.
